Privacy Policy

Privacy Statement

We collect personal information from our users in our letters.js application for the purpose of conducting advocacy. This modal answers some of your most frequently asked questions, and lets you know exactly how we're protecting the information you entrust to us. What personal information do you collect about me?
In our application we collect the following information about you:

- Name
- Email Address

When you visit our web site, we also collect the following pieces of metadata:
- Your IP address
- The type of operating system and browser you use
- What pages you visit on our site
- What links you click on

How do you use this information?
We use your first name, last name, and email address for the purpose of:
- Sending an email to your councillors on your behalf

We use your metadata for:
- Improvements to your mobile and desktop experiences
- Improvements to our website’s user interface

Sharing of Personal Information with Third Parties
We do not share your personal information with any third parties.
